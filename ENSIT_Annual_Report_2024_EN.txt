Director's Notes:
The dropout rate of 16.3% is alarmingly high and has increased
significantly compared to last year. Emergency intervention
plans are being discussed at the academic council level.
Budget execution stands at only 78%, leaving 1,364,000 TND
unspent — a serious concern given the infrastructure backlog.
IT equipment operability has dropped to 76%, with 34 machines
awaiting repair or replacement across 5 laboratories.
The Computer Engineering department now accounts for 38% of
total enrollment, creating a severe resource imbalance across
departments that must be addressed in the 2025 academic plan.
Sustainability performance is well below the network average,
with no solar installations and a waste sorting rate of only 18%.
